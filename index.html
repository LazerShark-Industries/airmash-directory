  1. Airmash.Online

    2. Airmash,the game that started it all,as a web browser game with Open Source code on Github. This game started in 2017, made by a programmer known as Five/Ninesigma or Tony. Growing more and more as it was announced on Hacker-News. The game is a mix of programmers and players from all over the world. The US CTF mode was where it all started, the legacy of airmash. Players flying their planes, gaining their skill, while chatting with awesome pilots. If they spoke their language of course. Come Along, Join the battle, Join the community, Most importantly be part of the Airmash Family!

  2. Starma.sh

    3. Starmash, a mod for airmash made by an awesome programmer known as, Bombita or Molesmalo. Starmash has themes and extensions that gives the client a one of a kind feeling. First started as a starwars version, to many volunteered themes from players like, Moz://a and xplay. As more and more themes came, more game-features came as-well. Some like the Prowler Radar, the infamous TPM - Targeting Players on the Minimap, to even crowns on the mini map for the players in 1st 2nd and 3rd. Beware Though, the more themes and features added, the harder it becomes to make the game smoother, meaning the bigger processor needed. Enjoy!
